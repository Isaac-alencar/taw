About characters

  • Rick Sanchez

    Rick Sanchez, also known as Rick (C-137), is the titular main protagonist of the science fiction adult animated comedy series Rick and Morty. He is a megagenius[4] scientist whose alcoholism and reckless, nihilistic behavior are sources of concern for his "daughter's" family, as well as the safety of their son, Morty. Upon the series' inception, Rick's mysterious origins and mental health were large sources of speculation. As time has gone on, more has been revealed about his character, his backstory explaining much of his behaviour. At the same time, Rick has continuously committed more heinous acts[5][6], certifying him as the series' anti-hero, bordering on antagonist.

  • Morty Smith

    Mortimer "Morty" Smith Sr. is the titular central protagonist of Rick and Morty. He is the grandson of Rick and is often forced to tag along on his various misadventures. Morty attends Harry Herpson High School along with his sister, Summer.[8] At the beginning of the series, Morty was shown to be an incredibly anxious and submissive teenage boy. However, after going on many so-called adventures with Rick, Morty has seemingly become more confident and his own person[9], independent from Rick, rather than a mere sidekick.

  • Summer Smith

    Summer Smith is the tritagonist of Rick and Morty, as a member of the Smith Family. She is the daughter of Jerry Smith and Beth Smith/Space Beth, the older sister of Morty Smith, and the mother of Naruto Smith. She currently acts as the older sister and the granddaughter of the Morty and Rick, respectively, from the Cronenberged Dimension and Dimension C-137.
